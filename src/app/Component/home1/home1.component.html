Social Networking Forum

Empowering Lives In Remote Communities

Established in 2010 by visionary social reformer Pramod Gopalrao Gaikwad, Social Networking Forum is committed to the holistic development of communities leaving in remote places of Maharashtra state in India. We believe in the power of water, education, and health as catalysts for positive change. So our mission is clear: to provide access to clean water, quality education, and essential healthcare services. Together, we are shaping a brighter future for our tribal brethren. Join us in our mission to uplift lives and build a more equitable society.
